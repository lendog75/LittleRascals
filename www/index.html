Our Philosophy

At Little Rascals Day Care all children will be given the opportunity to participate in a wide range of experiences from which to grow. It is our belief that children should be given freedom of choice whenever possible. We like kids to learn by doing things themselves and provide them with an endless amount of enthusiasm as well as tools to explore with.

Programs by Age

We are constantly changing our curriculum but maintain basic methods that we believe nurture children’s learning. Our methods are exemplified in the activities we do with the kids. Our activities are in the fields of Art, Science, Movement, Music, Outdoor play, and Sense related activities (smell, sight, feel)

Our Mission

Little Rascals Day Care and Learning Center Inc. provides high quality childcare to families with children between the ages of 30 months to 12 years of age. Children at Little Rascals Day Care are nurtured and supported in such a way that promotes positive self-esteem and provides the opportunity for optimal growth in all areas of development.

About

Family is very important to us at Little Rascals Day Care as it is owned and operated by a family partnership. We understand that leaving your child in the care of others can be a difficult decision. Our highly trained and dedicated staff will nurture and care for your child in a home-like environment to facilitate a smooth transition.
