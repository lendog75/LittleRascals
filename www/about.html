About Us

We would like to take this opportunity to welcome you and your family to Little Rascals Day Care and Learning Center Inc. The purpose of Little Rascals Day Care is to provide safe, high-quality childcare to families with children between the ages of 30 months TO 12 years old.

Family is very important to us at Little Rascals Day Care as it is owned and operated by a family partnership. We understand that leaving your child in the care of others can be a difficult decision. Our highly trained and dedicated staff will nurture and care for your child in a home-like environment to facilitate a smooth transition. LRDC has age appropriate curriculums, which are placed into 7 classrooms to meet your child’s individual needs. Each classroom takes into consideration the age, maturity level, and developmental stage of your child.

Our Mission

Little Rascals Day Care and Learning Center Inc. provides high quality childcare to families with children between the ages of 30 months to 12 years of age. Children at Little Rascals Day Care are nurtured and supported in such a way that promotes positive self-esteem and provides the opportunity for optimal growth in all areas of development.

LRDC will make reasonable accommodations for children with special needs in compliance with ADA (Americas with Disabilities Act).

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy at Little Rascals Day Care Inc. is that all children will be given the opportunity to participate in a wide range of experiences from which to grow. It is our belief that children should be given freedom of choice whenever possible
